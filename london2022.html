Surrogacy in Canada Ltd

Our Big Gay Journey

We are Lucy and Amy! Our story started with medicated IUI’s which resulted in negative pregnancy tests. After 3 unsuccessful rounds we moved onto IVF (ICSI). Surgeries, delays, cancelled cycles, many tears, heartbreak and three miscarriages later - a frozen embryo transfer gave us our rainbow baby, Nathaniel. That little rainbow baby is now a 16 month old toddler and as cliche as it sounds, he really was worth the wait. We were very lucky to have two embryos still frozen after Nathaniel’s cycle and we are currently pregnant with his little sibling due in February. We have shared our experience of trying to conceive as a same sex couple on our Instagram, Our Big Gay Journey - from how we chose our sperm donor to life with our amazing son. The ultimate goal of our account is to be a visible two Mum family; to normalise same sex families and help to make positive changes for the community.

My G Work

Founded by twin brothers Adrien and Pierre Gaubert eight years ago, myGwork is the business community for LGBTQ+ professionals, graduates, inclusive employers and anyone who believes in workplace equality. The award-winning company aims to empower the LGBTQ+ community by offering individual members a safe space where they can connect with inclusive employers, and find jobs and mentors. myGwork also organises free events, such as its annual virtual five-day conference WorkPride and career fair #WorkFair to help LGBTQ+ professionals connect with inclusive employers and other LGBTQ+ professionals/allies.
